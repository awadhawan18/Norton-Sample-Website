About Norton

Norton AntiVirus, created and distributed by Symantec Corporation, provides malware prevention and removal since 1991.Norton uses signatures and heuristics to identify viruses. Its features include e-mail spam filtering and phishing protection.

Norton Antivirus runs on Windows, Mac and Linux platforms. It also provide protection for Android devices.

As new threats emerge, Symantec immediately builds new protection updates and makes them available for download on a subscription basis.
